Privacy Policy for Resultarias:
At Resultarias, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. We do not collect any personal information or data from our users unless it is necessary for the proper functioning of the game. This includes, but is not limited to, information such as your name, email address, or location.

We use Unity Analytics tool to collect data about your in-game activity, such as your scores or progress. This data is used to improve the game and provide a better experience for our users. We do not share or sell this data to any third parties.

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of the game, except for Unity Analytics tool.

We do not have access to or collect any data about your device, such as its type or operating system.

In short, we collect only the necessary data through Unity Analytics tool to improve the game and provide a better experience for our users. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.

Your privacy is important to us and we will do everything in our power to protect it, while providing you the best experience with the game.
